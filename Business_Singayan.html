About ZARAP!


2024

ZARAP! is a local pizza restaurant located in Cagayan de Oro City. It was founded by Lloyd Vincent Singayan. From the Classic Cheese Pizza to BBQ Chicken Pizza, ZARAP! offers customers various delicious flavors at affordable prices.

ZARAP! also offers delivery and full-service catering since not only we had snacks but also having various meals for every customer.

Our Menu

Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni is a variety of spicy salami made from cured pork and beef seasoned with paprika and chili peppers.


Classic Cheese Pizza

Contains Mozzarella and cheddar cheese.


Garlic Beef Pizza

Our crispy crust is generously topped with savory garlic-infused tomato sauce, tender strips of seasoned beef, and a blanket of melted mozzarella cheese.


BBQ Chicken Pizza

Delightful combination of flavors that brings together the smoky, tangy taste of barbecue sauce with tender chicken and a variety of other delicious toppings.
